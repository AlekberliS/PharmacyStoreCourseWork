Our story

Established in 2017, PharmaStore emerges as the premier online pharmaceutical marketplace in South Asia, prominently serving the healthcare needs of communities in India. Backed by a suite of personalized marketing initiatives, data-driven insights, and impeccable service offerings, PharmaStore collaborates with 9,500 trusted suppliers and partners with over 250 esteemed pharmaceutical brands, catering to the wellness requirements of 3.5 million discerning customers across the region.

PharmaStore proudly hosts an expansive catalogue exceeding 1.2 million products, expanding dynamically to keep pace with the evolving healthcare landscape. Offering a comprehensive assortment across various categories, from essential medications to specialized healthcare equipment, PharmaStore ensures seamless access to a wide spectrum of healthcare solutions.
